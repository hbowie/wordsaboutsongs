You can find almost all of these songs (with the exception of “Christmas Bells” by John Gorka) in the “Words about Songs - Christmas” playlist on Apple Music.

  • “The Rebel Jesus” – Written and Performed by Jackson Browne

  • “Father Christmas” – Written by Ray Davies, performed by The Kinks

  • “Merry Christmas Baby” – Written by Charles Brown, Lou Baxter and Johnny Moore, Performed by Otis Redding

  • “Christmas Time Back Home” – Written by Ann Streeter, Performed by The Country Gentlemen

  • “Please Come Home for Christmas” – Written by Charles Brown and Gene Redd, performed by The Insight

  • “Christmas (Baby Please Come Home)” – Written by Ellie Greenwich, Jeff Barry and Phil Spector, Performed by Darlene Love, Produced by Phil Spector

  • “Holiday in Harlem” – Written by Chick Webb, Sung by Ella Fitzgerald

  • “Christmas Night in Harlem” – Written by Raymond Scott and Mitchell Parish, Performed by Louis Armstrong

  • “Christmas Bells” – Words by Henry Wadsworth Longfellow, Music and Performance by John Gorka

  • “Christmas Must Be Tonight” – Written by Robbie Robertson, Performed by The Band

  • “Have Yourself a Merry Little Christmas” – Written by Hugh Martin and Ralph Blane, Performed by Paul Ray

  • “Christmas Song by Dave Matthews” – Written and Performed by Dave Matthews

  • “Auld Lang Syne” – Written by Robert Burns, Performed by Andrew Bird

  • “The Little Drummer Boy” – Written by Katherine K. Davis, Henry Onorati and Harry Simeone

  • “My Favorite Things” – Written by Oscar Hammerstein and Richard Rodgers, Performed by Tony Bennett

  • “Star of Wonder” – Written by Terre Roche, Performed by The Roches

  • “Christmas Time’s A-Coming” – Written by Tex Logan, Performed by Emmylou Harris

  • “Boogie Woogie Santa Claus” – Written by Leon René, Performed by Mabel Scott

  • “The Christmas Song” – Written by Mel Tormé and Bob Wells, Performed by Diana Krall

  • “Santa Claus is Comin’ to Town” – Written by John Frederick Coots and Haven Gillespie, Performed by Bruce Springsteen and the E Street Band
